    As a Data Specialist

    Hi, I’m Eleonora Claessens, a data professional with a background in forensics and toxicology and over 3 years of hands-on experience in biosciences.

     

    When I’m not analysing data, I love to try new things and learn new skills. Whether it’s baking breads, building a table, brewing, growing mushrooms, building ecosystems, sewing, fixing gadgets or building my own website!

     

    My journey in data science began as a natural progression of my inquisitive nature and ability to find patterns and answers within complex datasets. Starting as a scientist and gradually moving into a more code heavy role. Now, I'm passionate about helping others streamline their data and gain answers to their questions. I do data cleaning and organization, data analysis using statistical calculations and data visualization.

     

    Currently, I use Excel or Google Sheets and expand with SQL and/or R when the project requires this.
