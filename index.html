    About Me

    Data Analyst

    Hi, I’m Eleonora Claessens, a data professional with a background in forensics and toxicology and over 3 years of hands-on experience in the biosciences industry.

     

    When I’m not analysing data, I love to try new things and learn new skills. Whether it’s baking breads, building a table, brewing, growing mushrooms, building ecosystems, sewing, fixing gadgets or building my own website!

     

    Throughout my career I become more and more aware of my general craving for knowledge, which is when I decided to fully move into data analysis. Now, I'm passionate about helping others streamline their data and seek answers to their questions. I do data cleaning and organization, data analysis using statistical calculations and data visualization. Currently, I use Excel or Google Sheets and expand with SQL and/or R when necessary.
